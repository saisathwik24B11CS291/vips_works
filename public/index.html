About The Application

VIPs helps workers find local job opportunities and helps employers discover trusted people for real work. It brings worker profiles, employer business pages, public posts, hiring invitations, notifications, and messaging into one simple network.

Local Job Discovery

Workers can browse posted jobs and invitations based on skills, categories, and location.

Business Networking

Employers build public profiles, grow followers, and connect with nearby workers.

Secure Communication

Private dashboards remain protected while public profile pages show only safe business information.

Help & Support

Answers, troubleshooting, and safety guidance for common platform tasks.

How do I create an account?

Choose Worker or Employer, complete signup, verify your email OTP, and continue to your dashboard.

How do I apply for jobs?

Workers can open the posted jobs tab, review job details, and use Apply.

How do I follow employers?

Open an employer public profile and select Follow or Unfollow.

How do invitations work?

Employers invite workers from the worker list. Workers receive a notification and can accept or reject the invite.

How do notifications work?

Notifications appear in the notification center and badge whenever important account or invitation activity happens.

How do I report a problem?

Use the contact details on this page and include your role, account email, and a short description.
